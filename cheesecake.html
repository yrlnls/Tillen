Cheese cake

Ingredients

  • 2 packages cream cheese, softened to room temperature
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon lemon extract
  • 1 container frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 1(9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust
  • 21 ounce can strawberry pie filling

Preparation Instruction

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine cream cheese, sugar, vanilla, and lemon extract in a large bowl; beat with an electric mixer until smooth and creamy.
  3. Fold in whipped topping
  4. Pour mixture into graham cracker crust and smooth evenly
  5. Top with pie filling and refregerate until firm, 2-3 hours
  6. Serve and enjoy!
